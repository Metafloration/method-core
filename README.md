## I. üß≠ The Ethical Compass: The Adressee Principle (Adressat-Prinzip)

Before starting, the search direction must be set. The **Adressee Principle** directs the metaflorative search unswervingly toward the well-being, autonomy, and dignity of the ultimate party affected by the emergence.

> **It transforms the question:** *‚ÄúWhat is possible?‚Äù* **into the question:** *‚ÄúWhat serves humanity and its flourishing?‚Äù*

The **simple rotation of perspective** is key: It is not *you* who has a problem ‚Äì **the problem has the Adressee!** (e.g., The shop doesn't need cookies; the user needs privacy.)

## II. üîÑ The Creation Rhythm: The E-A-K-E Process

Metafloration is surprisingly simple to apply‚Äîit is a clear cognitive movement, not complex theory. The process is a self-reproducing cycle:

### 1. **E**xploration of Reality (Applying the Compass)

* **Action:** Apply the **Adressee Principle** to identify all relevant **Addressees** (stakeholders) and their core needs (the fundus).
* **Example:** In the cookie dilemma: Shop (needs data), User (needs protection), Regulator (needs compliance).

### 2. **A**bstraction of Principles (Selecting the Seed)

* **Action:** Radically separate the core needs from their domain (e.g., separate **Trust** from **Consent Banner**). Reduce them to pure, combinable principles.
* **Example:** Data $\rightarrow$ **Measurability**; Protection $\rightarrow$ **Anonymity**; Compliance $\rightarrow$ **Simplicity**.

### 3. **K**ombination of Principles (Intentional Crossing)

* **Action:** Intentionally **interweave** the abstracted principles (often in a Dyad: Human-Human or Human-AI). The goal is to create creative tension that forces the resolution of the compromise.
* **The Question:** *How can these principles **reinforce each other** instead of hindering one another?*

### 4. **E**mergence of the New Reality (The Blossom)

* **Action:** Articulate the new **Reality** that emerges from the combination. The concrete form is unpredictable, but its **potential** is guaranteed to be of higher value, as it satisfies all the ethical constraints of the input.
* **Result:** A new class of solution, framework, or paradigm shift (e.g., **System Monitoring instead of User Tracking**).
